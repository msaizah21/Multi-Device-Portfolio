Event 1 - Raffle Winning Draw

People were really excited at the Zawaya walk when it was time for the raffle draw. Everyone gathered around eagerly, hoping that their ticket would be the one picked.

When the moment finally came, the draw was done very fairly. The person hosting the event called out the winning number while everyone whispered.

The happiness on the face of the winner was so infectious; it made everyone at the event happy too. It brought everyone together, creating a feeling of togetherness and making the event a big celebration.

Event 2 - Live Pizza Making with Kids

The children, adorned in aprons and chef hats, eagerly rolled dough and sprinkled toppings, learning not just culinary skills but also fostering teamwork and camaraderie in a lively, interactive setting.

It's a scene filled with joy - laughter fills the air as flour flies around, making learning an absolute blast. kids are all geared up in aprons and chef hats, having a great time rolling dough and adding their favorite toppings. They're not just learning cooking skills; they're also building teamwork and making friends in a fun and lively atmosphere.

Event 3 - Traditional Arabic Dance

Experience the enchanting world of traditional Arabic music and dance at Zawaya walk! Watch as talented performers take the stage, dressed in stunning and colorful costumes, moving gracefully to the rhythmic beats.

Their precise footwork and expressive movements tell stories of history and tradition, captivating everyone in the audience. Come and immerse yourself in the vibrant cultural heritage of the Arabic world through captivating dance routines and mesmerizing performances.
